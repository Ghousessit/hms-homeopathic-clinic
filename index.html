About Homeopathy

Homeopathy is a natural and holistic approach to medicine that uses highly diluted substances to stimulate the body's self-healing mechanisms. Developed in the late 18th century, it is based on the principle of "like cures like."

Homeopathic remedies are made from natural sources such as plants, minerals, and animals, and are prepared through a process of serial dilution. The goal is to treat the root cause of illness and promote overall health and balance.
